Connecting SAGE with Salesforce -
Outdure

  • A sync mechanism between Salesforce and Sage 300 ERP built by EST helps Outdure to automate the process of Order / Invoice / payment tracking.
  • Outdure uses Sage 300 ERP as their backend system to run their operations, financials etc.
  • Their sales and support reps using Salesforce for their day to day operations.
  • As soon as an order gets created in Sage ERP, EST’s sync picks up this order and creates an Opporutiy in Salesforce for the sales team to follow up and close the order.
  • Any new contact created in Salesforce gets transferred as Customer in Sage 300 by our sync utility.
  • Sales rep can see any Invoice been generated and even they can see any payment has been received or pending from clients using Salesforce without having an access to ERP system at the backend.
  • EST’s sync helps Outdure in their business process saving huge time in communication between operations, finance and sales team.
